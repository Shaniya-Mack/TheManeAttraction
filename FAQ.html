FAQ

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 3 to 5 business days once your order is processed.

Is delivery free?

Yes, we offer free standard delivery on all orders.

Can I style and dye my wig?

Yes, our human hair wigs can be styled and dyed. We recommend using heat tools on a low to medium setting to maintain the quality of the wig. Please note that synthetic wigs cannot be dyed and must be styled on a low heat setting.

Can I return or exchange a wig?

Yes, wigs can be returned once it is unworn, unwashed and not dyed. For hygiene purposes, we cannot accept returns on wigs that have been altered or worn.

How can return or exchange a wig?

You can bring the wig back to the store with the original receipt. If delivered, send picture of receipt to a customer representative who will provide detailed instructions on the process.

How to care for my wig?

Gently brush before and after wear, using a wide teeth comb or wig brush. Wash with a sulfate free shampoo and conditioner in cool water. For human hair wigs, do a deep conditioning treatment every week.
