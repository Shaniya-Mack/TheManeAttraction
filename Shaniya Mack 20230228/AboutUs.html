Mission Statement

At The Mane Attraction, our priority is to boost your confidence and beauty on a daily basis. We are dedicated to providing high-quality hair products and services that cater to your needs. Whether you are looking for your regular essentials or exploring new options, we are here to ensure your hair receives the attention it deserves. We believe that having amazing hair days should be a regular occurrence, and we are committed to making that happen.

Vission Statement

To be the leading provider of high quality, durable wigs that empowers individuals to express their uniqueness and confidence through their hair. Be Bold, Be Beautiful

History

The Mane Attraction established by Shaniya Mack in 2019 is a registered business in Trinidad that operates mainly in the hair and beauty industry and offers products that are ethically sourced to cater to their diverse customers. The Mane Attraction offers a wide range of quality human hair or synthetic wigs and extensions, as well as hair accessories.

Shaniya Mack is the visionary CEO of The Mane Attraction, known for her bold leadership and commitment to empowering beauty through quality hair solutions.
